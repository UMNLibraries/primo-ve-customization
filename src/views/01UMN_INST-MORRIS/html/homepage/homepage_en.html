What does Pounce Plus Search?

Pounce Plus searches across many, but not all, Briggs databases for articles in addition to print books, e-books, DVDs, and more! Please sign in to see the most complete set of results. For advanced article searching, it may still be best to search in specific database. Please Ask Us if you have any questions!

What does Pounce Plus not search?

Pounce Plus does not search EBSCO databases and certain subject-specific databases. New databases and resources are added frequently. Please Ask Us if you would like additional information.

What does Pounce Search?

Pounce (the library catalog) searches for books, e-books, print journals, e-journals, music scores, films, sound recordings, government publications, archival materials. In other words, items owned by or subscribed to by Briggs Library.

Pounce and UofM Campuses do not currently search for articles. Please enter the journal title from the citation.

Why "Pounce"?

The University of Minnesota Morris Cougars like to grasp and paw through important concepts and information, especially when conducting research. When stalking quality resources, Pounce is the purr-fect tool to find what you're looking for in the Rodney A. Brigg's Library collections.

Where can I get help?

Ask a Librarian: Contact us by e-mail, by chat or by phone. You're also invited to schedule an individual meeting with a librarian.
