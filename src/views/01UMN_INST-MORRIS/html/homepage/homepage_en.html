What does Articles & Books Search?

Articles & Books searches across many, but not all, Briggs databases for articles in addition to print books, e-books, DVDs, and more that are available at Briggs Library! Articles & Books does not search certain subject-specific databases. Please sign in to see the most complete set of results. For advanced article searching, it may still be best to search in a specific database. Please Ask Us if you have any questions!

What does Briggs Catalog search?

The Briggs Catalog searches for books, e-books, print journals, e-journals, music scores, movies, sound recordings, government publications, and archival materials available at Briggs Library. It does not search for articles. Please Ask Us if you would like additional information.

What does All UMN Campuses search?

All UMN Campuses searches for print materials available at any of the University of Minnesota campus libraries. These materials can be requested from the other campuses–sign in and select the “Get It” request option. Please Ask Us if you have any questions!

Where can I get help?

Ask a Librarian: Contact us by email, text, chat, or phone. You're also invited to schedule an individual meeting with a librarian.
