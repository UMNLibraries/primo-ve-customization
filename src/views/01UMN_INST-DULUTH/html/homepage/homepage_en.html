What does this search?

Millions of items including:

  • Articles
  • Books (electronic and print)
  • Government documents
  • Maps, archival, and University Digital Conservancy collections
  • Music, videos, DVDs, scores
  • Journals, magazines, and newspapers (electronic and print)

What doesn't this search?

  • UMedia Archive: Provides open access to digitized images, maps, manuscripts, video, audio, and other materials from across the University of Minnesota.
  • Archival Collection Guides: (or finding aids), are pointers to archival records, personal papers, or manuscripts. You can find brief summaries or detailed descriptions and inventories.
